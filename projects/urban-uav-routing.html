Adaptive Inter-UAV Network Routing for Urban Environments

Research / Networking Routing stability

Occlusion-Aware, Predictive Routing

Designed an occlusion-aware, predictive routing metric integrated into OLSR to maintain stable paths under high mobility and building blockages. It uses UAV GPS and city map geometry to estimate probabilistic Line-of-Sight (LoS) and bias an ETX-like link quality metric toward stable links. This reduces route flapping during topology changes in obstructed urban environments.

Outage Reduction: >40% → 5.6% LoS Prediction Horizon: 0.5s Platform: ns-3 Simulation
